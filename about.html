HI-LITE SOLUTIONS KEEPS IT CLEAN AND GREEN

At Hi-Lite Solutions our vision, values, mission and operating principles have been fine tuned over the past 20 years to guide us in the manufacturing of our full line of environmentally friendly products.

Hi-Lite Solutions, LLC is a US based manufacturer specializing in the development and manufacturing of environmentally friendly, powerful and effective specialty cleaning solutions for professionals under the brand name AEROGREEN.

AEROGREEN product lines have been developed over the past 25+ years to provide the most effective products trusted by professionals for use in a variety of industries. Our solutions replace the use of hazardous products and provide our customers with the superior cleaning they require along with the environmental safety we all demand.

The AEROGREEN product lines from Hi-Lite Solutions have a growing reputation for specific application technology, product performance, technical support and customer support, which have resulted in the expansion from existing US market to a global customer base.
